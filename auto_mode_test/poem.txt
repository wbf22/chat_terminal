A Quiet Loop

In the soft hum of late-night code,
A cursor walks a patient mile—
It writes, it reads, it keeps the road
Between the logic and the smile.

Each iteration tastes the air,
A gentle pulse of thought and time;
Variables line up with care,
A syntax rhythm, small and prime.

The file receives the wandering verse,
A pocket sky of words and light;
Saved like stars when nights converse,
A tiny beacon in the night.

Return to start, and let it be:
A poem tucked in memory.
